Global Warming

What is Global Warming?

Global Warming is the gradual increase in the yearly average temperature of the Earth's Atmosphere

What is the Greenhouse Effect?

The Greenhouse Effect is the process by which heat is trapped in the Earth's Lower Atmosphere by greenhouse gases.
