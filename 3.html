What to do to help solve Global Warming?

Here's some things you can be more concious about in your daily life to help reduce Global warming! :)

1.Motor vehicle use

-Purchase more fuel efficient cars and hybrids.You can also carpool and use public transport. Carpooling means to commute in a car with a group of people.

2. Eat your vegetables

-Eating more vegetables, fruits, whole grains, nuts, and less meat and dairy, can significantly lower your environmental impact. Producing plant-based foods generally results in fewer greenhouse gas emissions and requires less energy, land and water. In the long run, it's good for your body too!

3. Educate yourself and others

-Through education, it is important for everyone to be aware of the effects of global warming (ex recycling programs).
